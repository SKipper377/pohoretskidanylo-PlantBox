The Team Behind PlantBox

We are four engineers who turned a university project into a fully working smart-gardening ecosystem — hardware, mobile, web, and quality assurance all under one roof.

Our mission: make plant care effortless

PlantBox was born from a simple frustration — keeping plants alive is harder than it should be. We combined IoT sensors, a cross-platform mobile app, and a community-driven web platform into one seamless experience. Every line of code, every sensor reading, every UI decision serves one goal: healthier plants with less effort.

Meet the Team

Four specialists, one product. Each of us owns a critical layer of PlantBox — so when you reach out, you get answers from the person who actually built that part.

QA Lead

Andrii is the reliability backbone of PlantBox. If PlantBox feels smooth and dependable, that's Andrii's work in action.

Mobile App

Stanisław Priadko

Stanisław is the reason PlantBox feels great to use on your phone. Hardware & IoT

Daniil Sliapets

Daniil is where software meets the physical world. He engineered the ESP32-based sensor node that lies at the heart of PlantBox — wiring up temperature, humidity, and soil sensors, writing the firmware, and building the cloud pipeline that pushes real-time data to both the mobile app and the web dashboard. No data, no PlantBox.
